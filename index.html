About Us

We believe hiking has the power to transform lives. Sometimes, finding the perfect hike can be hard. Choosing and planning a hike should be fun, but often the process is frustrating and the information is hard to find. Not anymore!

The Hiking Club is an online platform that turns trail information into a range of personalised, self-guided hikes designed to suit your preferences, fitness and interests.
